## About

I build AI systems that go beyond notebooks — from a published iOS app on the App Store to an IEEE-accepted paper presented at WiDS 2026. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, data engineering, and real-world deployment. Currently finishing my graduation project: a Digital Twin for Riyadh Metro combining multi-model forecasting, an AI chatbot, and an automated Lost & Found system. I'm curious by nature, driven by impact, and I care about code that actually ships.
